# ZEVA Clinic Workflow Guide

## Overview
This is a comprehensive documentation library that explains the complete clinic workflow step by step for users. The guide covers all essential aspects of clinic management within the ZEVA platform.

## Features
- **Step-by-step documentation** of the complete clinic workflow
- **Visual screenshots** for each section (stored in the public folder)
- **Interactive navigation** through different workflow sections
- **Business-focused header** with ZEVA branding
- **Responsive design** that works on all devices

## Workflow Sections Covered

1. **User Registration** - Clinic account creation process
2. **Login Process** - Secure access to the clinic dashboard
3. **Dashboard Overview** - Navigation through all clinic modules
4. **Manage Health Center** - Clinic profile management
5. **Create Agent** - Adding doctors and staff with role definitions
6. **Create Lead** - Lead management and conversion process
7. **Setup Operation** - Operational configuration (rooms, services)
8. **Appointment Booking** - Scheduling with doctors, rooms, and time slots
9. **Scheduled Appointments** - Managing upcoming appointments
10. **Job Posting** - Recruitment and job management
11. **Patient Registration** - Patient management system
12. **Write Blog** - Content creation and publishing
